Day 5

What are your experiences with humility?

Reflect on experiences in your life when you witnessed an act of humility. This could be an act of humility you've done, or that someone has done; it's whatever you want. Describe that experience in detail. How did you feel? Why was that experience memorable for you? As you reflect on this experience, consider how your life would be happier if you created more of those experiences in your life.

Day 6

Practice Humility
