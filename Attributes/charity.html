Day 5

What are your experiences with charity?

Reflect on experiences in your life when you witnessed an act of charity. This could be an act of charity you've done, or that someone has done for you; it's whatever you want. Describe that experience in detail. How did you feel? Why was that experience memorable for you? As you reflect on this experience, consider how your life would be happier if you created more of those experiences in your life.

Day 6

Practice Charity

There's no better way to learn what charity is and how it can bless your life than actually practicing it. After watching the following videos, think of an act of charity you could do for someone else, make a plan, and then do it!
