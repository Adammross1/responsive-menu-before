Day 5

What are your experiences with hope?

Reflect on experiences in your life when you witnessed hope. This could be an experience with hope that you've had, or someone else; it's whatever you want. Describe that experience in detail. How did you feel? Why was that experience memorable for you? As you reflect on this experience, consider how your life would be happier if you created more of those experiences in your life.

Day 6

Practice hope

There's no better way to learn what hope is and how it can bless your life than actually practicing it. After watching the following videos, think of how you could help someone else feel more hope, make a plan, and then do it!
