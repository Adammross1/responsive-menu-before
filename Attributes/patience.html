Day 5

What are your experiences with patience?

Reflect on experiences in your life when you witnessed an act of patience. This could be an act of patience you've done, or that someone else has done; it's whatever you want. Describe that experience in detail. How did you feel? Why was that experience memorable for you? As you reflect on this experience, consider how your life would be happier if you created more of those experiences in your life.

Day 6

Practice Patience

There's no better way to learn what patience is and how it can bless your life than actually practicing it. After watching the following videos, think of an act of patience you could do for someone else, make a plan, and then do it!
